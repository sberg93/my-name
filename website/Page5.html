Siberia, 1945



“I met my husband in 1939 really, while we were traveling, but we didn’t marry until 1945. 


Every little place, they put up a different trailor, so his trailor was always with his family next to our trailor. So I knew him for quite a long time. 

The Polish Government said to the Russians, ”they have to let us go”, because we are not spies- they had to let us go back home. A little later we gave birth to my first son, Eli.
