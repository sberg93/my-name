Poland, 1920



“I was born in 1920, in Poland, in a city called Bilgoraj.


My mother was a business lady. She used to go into small towns and she used to be a business lady- so my father was a business man too. I had a sister and a brother- Fradle and Elah.

I was the oldest in the whole family. I took care of the children when they were very young because my mother used to go away on business so I was really taking care of them. I was very young and took over the house and really helped out in the house. We had two rooms for my mother, my father, my sisters and brothers”.
