Poland, 1945



“We went back to Poland. From Poland we went to Germany. From Germany we went to this country. 


My husband had family in Hartford, Connecticut. When we came to America, we came to my husband’s uncle, Goldschmidt. He took us down from the ship.

I went with my husband and my baby, Eli., that’s all. I settled in Hartford, Connecticut, and I was there the whole time, until I was sick and came to New York”.
